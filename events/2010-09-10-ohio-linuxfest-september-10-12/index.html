Ohio Linuxfest September 10-12

    Ohio Linuxfest starts this Friday, September 10 at the Greater Columbus Convention Center. Various events occur that day, but the main part of the conference is Saturday, which consists of talks from 9am to 7:30pm and an expo with many Linux-related organizations. The Open Source club has a table at the expo, so please stop by.
